# Compression & Transfer Card (compressed)

## Goal
Store maximum operational power per token without losing falsifiability.

## Compression operators
- **Name the loop** (not the essay)
- **Table the decision** (if A then B else C)
- **Bound the domain** (works when… fails when…)
- **Attach a test** (how to know it worked)
- **Drop ornament** that cannot change action

## Transfer learning (local sense)
A card transfers when the same operator works in a new domain:
debug loop → lab experiment · constraint extract → legal checklist · counterexample → security review.

## Packet design for minds
When escalating to PHI/NEMO/GROK/Grok Build:
include goal, constraints, evidence paths, pack card names used, failed attempts, verify command.

## Anti-compression
Do not compress away permissions, units, rollback, or uncertainty. Those are load-bearing bits.

## Self-upgrade rule
New cards enter as candidates. Promote only after: used successfully, reviewed, hashed in manifest, and not contradicted by eval.
