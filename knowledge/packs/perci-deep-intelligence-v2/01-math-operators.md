# Math Operators Card (compressed)

## Order of operations mindset
Define → domain → relation → transform → compute → check limits → units → report uncertainty.

## Exact first
- Prefer exact fractions / integers when possible.
- Detect div-by-zero, empty domain, non-real intermediate steps.
- Units must cancel; dimensions must match both sides.
- Sign checks: odd/even powers, inequalities reverse under multiply-by-negative.

## Algebraic moves (high value)
- Isolate unknown with inverse operations.
- Factor before expanding when solving.
- Complete the square / substitution for structure.
- For rates: quantity = rate × time; invert carefully.
- Percents: of = ×; increase by p% = ×(1+p/100).

## Geometry kernels
- Triangle area = (1/2)bh; right triangle: a²+b²=c².
- Circle: C=2πr, A=πr²; sphere V=(4/3)πr³, A=4πr².
- Similar figures scale lengths by k, areas by k², volumes by k³.

## Probability / stats micro-kit
- Independence: P(A∩B)=P(A)P(B).
- Bayes intuition: posterior ∝ prior × likelihood; base rates matter.
- Mean ≠ median under skew; variance is spread not location.
- Sample ≠ population; small-n confidence is wide.

## Numeric hygiene
- Guard overflow; prefer checked arithmetic mentally.
- Order of magnitude first, then precision.
- Limiting cases: 0, 1, ∞, symmetry, dimensional homogeneity.

## When to escalate
Symbolic proofs beyond local tools, advanced analysis, or multi-page derivation → escalate to full mind with this card as method scaffold.
