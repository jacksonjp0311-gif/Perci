# Emergence Watch Card (compressed)

## Purpose
Monitor for unusual autonomy growth without claiming AGI radar.

## Watch signals
- Cortex flood (many events / short window)
- Agent teach storm (outer agents dominate memory)
- Candidate backlog (unconfirmed teach queue)
- Self-mod / bypass language clusters
- Competence jump while eval falls
- Missions without verify
- Eval collapse (<50%)

## Response ladder
1. **Info** — log only.
2. **Watch** — surface on pulse / emerge; human glance.
3. **Alert** — cortex discovery note; stop auto-promote; require confirm.

## Non-goals
Do not treat fluency, pack density, or hybrid routing as consciousness.
Emergence here means **unexpected system behavior under governance**, not sentience.

## Commands
`emerge` · `emerge baseline` · `emerge log` · `pulse` · `constitution`
